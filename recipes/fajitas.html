Fajitas

Description

Steak might get most of the sizzle when it comes to the subject of fajitas, but the world's poultry fans love the chicken version of this crazy popular dish. Like its beefy cousin, chicken fajitas are extra magical if you follow a few simple rules. We're breaking it down, and also including plenty of our top-rated chicken fajita recipes.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Bell peppers
  • Onion
  • Fajita mix
  • Salsa
  • Flour tortillas
  • Cooking oil
  • Grated cheese

Steps

  1. Fry the chickem in a pan with some oil
  2. Slice up the peppers and onions
  3. Chuck some fajita mix onto the chicken and coat
  4. Add the chopped veggies and stiry fry
  5. Add a couple of blobs of salsa and stir through
  6. Cook until chicken is done, veggies are soft and everything is sizzling
  7. Put some salsa and cheese on a tortilla then scoop on the chicken and veg
  8. Wrap and eat
